Introductions

Launched on 6/14/2015, this project's main purpose is to demonstrate Taiwan Railway (TRA) real-time schedule, which can be seen in most TRA stations, with much flexibility and better layout. We hope it would become helpful as it can be displayed in visitor centers or stores near TRA stations to remind visitors about their incoming trains.

The design itself was inspired from Japanese private railway company: Kintetsu (近鉄). When I visited Nara in 2013, I was amazed by Kintetsu's PIDS. It was stylish, smooth and easy to read, and it has been the best PIDS design in my opinion since then. When deciding which
